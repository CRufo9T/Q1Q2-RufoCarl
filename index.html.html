Porco Rosso

Porco Rosso is a animated film from studio Ghibli created in 1992. The story is about an italian WW1 ex-fighter ace pilot named Marco Pagot. When he was into a dogfight, evryone died except for him, but in exchange he got a curse that turned him into a pig. He now lives his life bounty hunting pirates, but then Donald Curtus shoots down his plane, and he needs to repair it. He then went to Fio Piccolo, who repaired his plane. Then Porco Rosso settled the score with Donald Curtis in a fight.
